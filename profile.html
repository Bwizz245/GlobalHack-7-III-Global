    How To Write a Resume

    Summary: Enter a brief description of your professional background. You can choose to highlight specific skills, knowledge, or industry experience.

    Skills: Enter 6-8 skill highlights you'd like to feature.

    Experience: Enter details about what you did in your previous jobs. Start with your responsibilities, but also include results and achievements.

    Education and Training: Enter any colleges, universities or training programs that you have attended.

    Activities and Honors: Enter any professional organizations that you are involved in.
