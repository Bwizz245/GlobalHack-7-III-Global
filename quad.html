    Finding Jobs Based on Skill and Fluency

    A test of experience and fluency in english will place each individual on a four quadrant grid. Top right corner is fully fluent and highly skilled. Bottom left is no english and no training or experience. Using this grid, we can specify and narrow down the amount and difficulty of their recomended classes and training.

    Benefits include:

    • Specilized and specific courses
    • Note starting points and track progress
    • Individualized, direct attention to each individuals needs
    • Provides an accurate depiction of where the individual stands
