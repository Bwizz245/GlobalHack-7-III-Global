About III Global

Our Mission Statement

At III Global we provide easily accessible resources designed to assist foreign-born individuals. Through housing accommodations, education, and a starting place to begin their careers, we aim to make the community a more welcoming and accessible place.

Our Vision Satement

We see the future holding many opportunities for foreign-born individuals. Our goal is to allow everyone a fair opportunity to lead their lives in their communities. We envision a place where everyone has the resources they need to do this, not only removing possible barriers but allowing everyone’s individual experiences to enhance their journey.
