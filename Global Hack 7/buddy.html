    • Introducing The Buddy System

    Everyone needs a buddy

    Here at III Global, we value your comfort and want to make sure you get situated as soon and as comfortably as possible. To assist with this we created the buddy system. When you create an account, we have you enter information such as age, gender, language, and country of origin. We plan on hundreds of individuals creating accounts like these. We will then use this data, which is kept private, to match you with other individuals that have similar backgrounds. By creating groups of alike people, they can navigate their individual situations, together.

    Benefits of creating groups like this include:

    • Ability to communicate with each other
    • Shared transportation/car pool
    • Shared/more affordable housing
    • Creating immediate bonds between people who may not have connections with their community
